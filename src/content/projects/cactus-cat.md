Brand identity for a conceptual craft beer company inspired by the mythological Southwestern cactus cat — a creature known for its love of fermented cactus juice.

Typography from Emigre's Platelet (inspired by California license plates). Warm desert color gradients. Illustrative stretching cactus-covered cat logo. Label for "Cactus Bloom Prickly Pear Pale Ale" (5.5% ABV, 12 fl oz) meets industry packaging standards: barcode, government warning, recycling symbol.

Brand applications: t-shirt, coasters, taproom environmental branding.
