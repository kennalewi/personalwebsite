Capstone exhibition at Tabula Rasa exploring human-computer connection through memetics. "Memesis" is a portmanteau of internet memes (units of cultural information that spread through imitation) and mimesis (Ancient Greek: imitation/representation). The work traverses cultural language and its connections to online space.

Wall text: *"Computers are not only mimicries of us, but we are, in effect, mimicries of computation... our cells act as individual Turing machines."*

Rooted in the artist's experience as a digital native shaped by early/mid-2000s internet culture — fan forums, self-portraits, original characters.
